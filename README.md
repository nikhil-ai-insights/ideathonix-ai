## 📖 Overview

Traditional idea generators stop at giving you an idea. **Ideathonix AI goes further.**

It combines AI-driven ideation, architecture planning, implementation guidance, and technical recommendations into **one seamless workflow** — producing a complete, development-ready blueprint instead of just a paragraph of inspiration.

Built for **founders, innovators, students, and hackathon teams** who want to move from concept to build with confidence and speed.
